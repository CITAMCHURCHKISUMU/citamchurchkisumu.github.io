About Us

Our History

Christ Is The Answer Ministries (CITAM) Kisumu is a vibrant Christian church located in Kisumu, Kenya.As part of the broader CITAM network, the church is committed to spreading the message that "Christ is the Answer" to all of life's questions and challenges. Formerly known as Kisumu Pentecostal Church-CITAM Kisumu was founded in 2001 and was established in Tom Mboya Estate before moving to its current location in Milimani Estate along Kisumu Nairobi Highway,opposite New Nyalenda Railways Estate.
